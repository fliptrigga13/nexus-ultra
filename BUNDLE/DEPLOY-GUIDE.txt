VEILPIERCER — DEPLOYMENT GUIDE
========================================

Congratulations on your purchase of VeilPiercer.

QUICK START:
1. Open index.html in any modern browser.
2. Enter your Personal Access Token when prompted.
3. Point the connection to your NEXUS ULTRA server.

INTEGRATION (Pro/Agency):
- Drop 'veilpiercer.py' (Python) or 'veilpiercer.js' (Node.js) into your agent folder.
- Follow the code examples in the SDK files to pipe real-time telemetry to your Hub.

Email support@veilpiercer.com for technical integration help.
