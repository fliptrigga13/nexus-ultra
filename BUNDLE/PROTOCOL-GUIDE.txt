VEILPIERCER — PROTOCOL GUIDE
========================================

OPERATIONAL MODES:

◈ NOMINAL MODE
The standard operating environment. All agents perform within 
established baseline constraints. Focus: Stability and Accuracy.

◈ LITE MODE
Low-latency, lightweight reasoning. 30-40% faster response 
times with slightly reduced complexity depth. 
Best for: Chat and Basic Analysis.

◈ GOD MODE (Restricted)
Full agent autonomy and maximum reasoning depth. 
Caution: High resource consumption.
Best for: Scaling and Critical System Repair.
