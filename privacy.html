Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · PIPEDA-Compliant · VeilPiercer by Lauren Flipo

1. What We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required to process your purchase:

  • Name and email address (via Stripe checkout)
  • Payment information (processed and stored by Stripe — we never see your card details)
  • Purchase timestamp and transaction ID

2. What We Do NOT Collect

VeilPiercer runs 100% locally on your hardware. We do not collect:

  • Your AI agent activity or swarm data
  • Your local files, logs, or memory databases
  • Browsing behaviour or analytics (no tracking pixels, no Google Analytics)
  • Any data from your local infrastructure

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used solely to deliver your access credentials and product updates. We do not sell, share, or rent your information to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Purchase records are stored securely via Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant). Your email is stored in our access-token system, protected by server-side authentication.

5. Cookies

The VeilPiercer website uses session cookies for authentication purposes only. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise these rights, email: support@veil-piercer.com

7. Third-Party Services

We use Stripe for payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data.

8. Contact

Privacy inquiries: support@veil-piercer.com
